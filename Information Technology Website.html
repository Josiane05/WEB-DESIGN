About AUCA

The Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA) known as MUDENDE is a Seventh-day Adventist Institution of Higher Learning which has a philosophy to operate on the basis of Seventh-day Adventist worldview:"God is the Creator and Sustainer of the universe and the source of true knowledge."

AUCA: A Modern-Day Calling, Rooted in Israel's Mission

From the very beginning, God called Israel to be a light to the nations--a people set apart to reveal His character, uphold His truth, and serve as a blessing to the world. Adventist University of Central Africa(AUCA)carries that same divine calling today.

Description of IT Faculty

The Faculty of Information Technology is a unique Faculty in the School of Sciences at AUCA, which is comprised of several Engineering and Sciences Departments.


Programs are designed to provide hands-on skills training. They focus on the technology of design, construction, implementation, and maintenance of hardware and software components of modern systems and computer-controlled equipment.


Departments in AUCA:
  • Software Engineering
  • Networking
  • Information Technology
